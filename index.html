Rules Of The Game

    In this game each player tries to eliminate orbs of his opponent. Now a window appears displaying a 8X8 grid.
    The player who starts the game puts green orbs and the other puts blue orbs. Each player can put the orbs in a blank cell or in a cell containing orbs of his colour by clicking on the desired cell.
    The player whoever completely eliminates the orbs of his opponent wins the game.
    When the game ends, a window opens displaying the winner.
